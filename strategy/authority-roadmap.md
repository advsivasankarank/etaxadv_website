# Authority Building Roadmap — E Tax Advisors Private Limited

## Objective
Transform E Tax Advisors from a mid-tier advisory firm into a recognised authority in tax, legal and compliance advisory. Authority is built through proof, visibility, recognition and trust signals — not through website design alone.

## Current Authority Score: 4/10
This roadmap targets 8/10 within 24 months.

---
