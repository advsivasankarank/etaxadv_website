# Thought Leadership Content Plan — E Tax Advisors Private Limited
## 12-Month Publishing Calendar

### Objective
Position K. Sivasankaran and E Tax Advisors as authoritative voices in tax, legal and compliance advisory. Content targets business decision-makers who search for guidance when they receive notices, face compliance decisions, or evaluate advisory firms.

### Publishing Cadence
- 2 articles per month (24 total for the year)
- 1 client-facing compliance alert per quarter (4 total)
- 1 newsletter per quarter (4 total)
- LinkedIn posts: 2 per week (repurposed from articles)
