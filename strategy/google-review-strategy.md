# Google Review & Reputation Strategy — E Tax Advisors Private Limited

## Current State Assessment
- 3,000+ clients served | 15+ years in business
- Reviews currently exist but are not systematically collected
- Average rating and review count unknown (needs audit)

## Target (12 Months)
- **50+ Google reviews** (from current baseline)
- **Average rating: 4.5+ stars**
- **Response rate: 100%** — every review acknowledged
- **Review content quality:** Specific, detailed, service-referencing

## Why Reviews Matter for E Tax Advisors
1. **Trust signal** — A business with 50+ reviews is perceived as established and reliable
2. **Local SEO** — Reviews are the #2 local ranking factor (after GMB completeness)
3. **Conversion** — Businesses with 50+ reviews see 4x+ enquiry rates on Google
4. **Premium positioning** — Detailed, professional reviews signal premium service (vs. 1-line "good CA" reviews for commodity providers)

## Strategy Overview

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1)

#### Audit Existing Reviews
- [ ] Check Google Business Profile for all existing reviews
- [ ] Export review data: count, average rating, common themes, gaps
- [ ] Check other platforms: Justdial, Sulekha, IndiaMart (if listed)
- [ ] Document review sentiment themes

#### Set Up Review Infrastructure
- [ ] Create a short, trackable link to Google review page: `g.page/r/etaxadv/review`
- [ ] Generate QR code for in-office display
- [ ] Add review link to email signatures
- [ ] Add review CTA to WhatsApp auto-reply

#### Respond to All Existing Reviews
- [ ] Thank every reviewer by name
- [ ] Reference specific feedback
- [ ] For any negative reviews: apologise, explain resolution, invite offline discussion
- [ ] Ensure all responses are professional and consistent

### Phase 2: Systematic Collection (Months 2-6)

#### Trigger-Based Review Requests
The key to getting quality reviews is asking at the right moment. These are the highest-NPS moments:

| Trigger Moment | Timing | Channel | Expected Conversion |
|---|---|---|---|
| After a GST notice is resolved positively | Within 48 hours of outcome | WhatsApp + email | 30-40% |
| After an income tax assessment closes without addition | Within 48 hours | WhatsApp + email | 30-40% |
| After a compliance filing is completed on time | Within 24 hours | WhatsApp | 15-20% |
| After a successful labour inspection | Within 24 hours | WhatsApp | 40-50% |
| After trust/NGO registration is approved | Within 48 hours | WhatsApp + email | 50-60% |
| After a consultation where client expressed satisfaction | Within 24 hours | WhatsApp | 10-15% |
| After a quarterly compliance review | Same day | In-person/email | 20-30% |

#### Request Template (WhatsApp)
> "Dear [Client Name],
>
> Thank you for trusting us with your [specific matter — e.g., GST notice representation]. We are glad that [specific outcome — e.g., the matter was resolved without any additional demand].
>
> If you found our service helpful, would you consider sharing your experience on Google? Your review helps other business owners make informed decisions.
>
> https://g.page/r/etaxadv/review
>
> Thank you for your support."

#### Request Template (Email — for larger matters)
> Subject: We would appreciate your feedback
>
> Dear [Client Name],
>
> Thank you for engaging E Tax Advisors for [specific service/matter]. We value our relationship with you and are committed to delivering structured, professional advisory.
>
> If you have a moment, we would be grateful if you could share your experience on Google. Your honest feedback helps us improve and helps other businesses find the right advisory support.
>
> [Review Link]
>
> Thank you for your trust.

#### Review Station at Office
- Place a tablet/display at the reception with the review form open
- Train reception staff to invite clients to leave a review after meetings
- Add "Leave us a review" card to client welcome kits and engagement closure folders

### Phase 3: Incentivisation & Scale (Months 3-12)

#### Review Response Protocol

| Rating | Response Strategy |
|---|---|
| 5-star, detailed | Thank personally, reference the specific matter, mention the team member who handled it |
| 5-star, brief | Thank warmly, invite them to share more details, mention other services |
| 4-star | Thank sincerely, acknowledge the feedback, invite suggestions |
| 3-star | Apologise, address specific concern, explain resolution or improvement, invite offline discussion |
| 1-2 star | Immediate personal call from Managing Director. Understand issue. Resolve. THEN respond publicly |

#### Topic Coverage in Reviews
Aim for reviews that reference these topics (spread across all reviews):
- Specific service used (GST, income tax, labour law, trust, litigation)
- Professional knowledge and expertise
- Responsiveness and communication
- Ease of process (especially for registration/startup clients)
- Value for money
- Comparison to previous advisor experience (implicit differentiator)

#### What to Do With Reviews
- Add best reviews as website testimonials (with permission)
- Quote reviews in consultation process pages
- Share positive reviews on LinkedIn (with permission)
- Use review content to identify service gaps and improvement areas
- Mention review count as social proof in proposals: "Join 3000+ businesses who trust us"

### Phase 4: Ongoing (Month 6+)

#### Monthly Review Meeting
- First Monday of every month: review new reviews, response quality, collection rate
- Track: reviews collected vs. target, average rating, response rate
- Adjust collection strategy based on which trigger moments generate most reviews

#### Quarterly Client NPS Survey
- Send a simple 1-question survey: "How likely are you to recommend us?" (0-10)
- Promoters (9-10): Immediately request Google review
- Passives (7-8): Ask for feedback on improvement
- Detractors (0-6): Personal call from MD

#### Negative Review Protocol
- [ ] Acknowledge within 24 hours
- [ ] Do NOT get defensive
- [ ] Apologise for the specific issue
- [ ] Explain what happened (briefly, no excuses)
- [ ] Describe what has been fixed
- [ ] Invite offline discussion for resolution
- [ ] Follow up to see if they would update their review after resolution

#### Sample Negative Review Response
> "Dear [Name], thank you for your honest feedback. We take your concern seriously. We have reviewed what happened with your [matter] and have [specific corrective action taken]. We have also updated our process to ensure this does not recur. We would welcome the opportunity to discuss this further and make it right. Please contact [person] at [email/phone] directly."
